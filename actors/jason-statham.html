Filmography Follicle Facts

Jason Statham’s dossier finally has a confirmed entry: his first kiss, last bald moment combo hits hyperspeed in John Carpenter’s Ghosts of Mars (2001). The Red Planet romp delivers both the smooch and the shine in one neon-drenched package.

First Kiss: Ice Train Rendezvous

During the climactic train escape, Statham’s Sgt. Jericho Butler steals a desperate, dust-coated kiss with Lieutenant Melanie Ballard. It’s a quick spark in the middle of a Martian massacre, and it stands as his first on-screen lip-lock.

Last Bald: Chrome Dome Combat

The same sequence doubles down on his trademark gleam. Helmet off, scalp reflecting the strobes, Butler wrestles possessed miners while the train barrels through Shining Canyon. It’s peak Statham: bald, brawling, and bathed in red light.
